# Trade-exposed baseline-adjusted (TEBA) facility list

Returns the bundled list of facilities declared as TEBA under the 2023
reform. TEBA facilities are eligible for softer baseline treatment
reflecting international competitiveness.

## Usage

``` r
cer_safeguard_teba_facilities()
```

## Value

A `cer_tbl` of TEBA facilities.

## References

Commonwealth of Australia (2023). *National Greenhouse and Energy
Reporting (Safeguard Mechanism) Rule 2015* as amended by the Safeguard
Mechanism (Crediting) Amendment Rule 2023. TEBA definition and
declaration process.
